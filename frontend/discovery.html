What these metrics mean
Pass rate — % of responses clearing all 7 policy checks. Above 95% = healthy.
Failed — responses that broke a policy rule (guarantee language, wrong escalation). Need human review.
Failure modes — distinct error patterns found. More early on is normal. Flatline = you've mapped them all.
Annotated — % of traces human-reviewed. Higher = more confident the failure taxonomy is complete.
Helpful score — user rating (1–3). 1 = not helpful, 3 = resolved. Low scores flag quality issues.
